What is Chu?

Chu is an ancient state at the current mid-south mainland China. It was a free state, but it got destoryed by Qin, the first Chinese Empire. The Chu people values their freedom and have prophecy : “It must be Chu people who destory Qin even there are only three Chu families left”. Its glorious and repectful noble - Xiang Ji led the revolt against Qin and destoryed Empire Qin.
